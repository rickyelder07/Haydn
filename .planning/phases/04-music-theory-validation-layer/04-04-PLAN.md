    3. Do NOT change note colors based on scale conformance -- that would conflict with velocity coloring and selection highlighting. The ROW highlighting is sufficient.

    Update the PianoRollCanvasProps interface to include the new prop.

    Update the useEffect dependency array to include scalePitchClasses.

    IMPORTANT: Keep the highlight very subtle (low alpha). The piano roll should not look cluttered. The existing grid, notes, and selection highlighting take priority visually.
